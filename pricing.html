Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between monthly and annual billing?
With annual billing, you save 20% compared to monthly billing. Annual plans are billed for the entire year upfront.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?
Yes, we offer special pricing for qualified nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team for more information.
How does the 5-member limit work on the Business plan?
The Business plan includes up to 5 team members. If you need more than 5 members, you can add additional seats for an extra fee per user per month, or consider the Enterprise plan for unlimited team members.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and M-Pesa for supported regions. Enterprise customers can also pay via invoice.
