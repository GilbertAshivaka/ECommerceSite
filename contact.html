Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent support requests, our team aims to get back to you within 4 hours.
Can I schedule a product demo?
Absolutely! Fill out the contact form and select "Sales inquiry" from the dropdown menu. Mention that you're interested in a demo, and our sales team will reach out to schedule a personalized demonstration.
Do you offer phone support?
Yes, phone support is available for Business and Enterprise customers. Free plan users have access to email support and our knowledge base.
Where can I find documentation and tutorials?
Our comprehensive documentation, including tutorials and guides, is available in our Help Center. You can access it by logging into your account and clicking on "Help" in the menu, or by visiting help.nucleus.com.
